Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and sign up with your email address. You'll be asked to choose a username and can optionally set a display name and profile picture.

How does the muscle rank system work?

Each of your 14 muscle groups earns XP when you log lifts. As you accumulate XP, your muscles progress through ranks: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, and Master. Your body diagram updates with rank colors so you can visualize your progress.

How do I track a run?

Go to the Record tab and start a run. The app uses GPS to track your route, distance, pace, and duration in real time. When you finish, your run is saved and can be shared with friends.

How do I add friends?

Go to the Social tab and search for users by username. Tap "Add Friend" to send a request. Once accepted, you'll see each other's public activities in your feed and can compare stats.

How do I delete my workout data?

Go to your Profile, tap "Edit Profile", and scroll to the bottom. The "Remove All Lifts" button will permanently delete all your lift data and reset muscle progress.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at eathandueno@gmail.com and we will process your request promptly.

Is my data private?

Yes. Only activities you mark as public are visible to friends. Your workout details, muscle progress, and personal information are private by default. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
