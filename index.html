Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Muscles Social Fitness ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, username, and display name.

Fitness Data: We collect workout data you voluntarily enter, including exercises, sets, reps, weights, and muscle progress.

Location Data: If you use the run tracking feature, we collect GPS location data only while you are actively recording a run. We do not track your location in the background.

Photos: If you choose to set a profile picture or use the camera overlay feature, we access your camera or photo library only when you initiate those actions.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain your account and fitness tracking features
  • To display your workout progress and muscle rank achievements
  • To enable social features such as friend connections, activity feeds, and stat comparisons
  • To display your run routes on maps within the app

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database platform with enterprise-grade security. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your fitness data is only visible to:

  • You (all your data)
  • Your friends (activities you mark as public)
  • Other users (only your public profile: username, display name, and avatar)

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Authentication and data storage
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (subject to Google's privacy policy)
  • Apple MapKit / Google Maps: Map display for run routes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and view all data we store about you
  • Delete your workout data at any time via the app settings
  • Delete your account entirely by contacting us
  • Opt out of making any activity public

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
